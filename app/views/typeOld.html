The Blue Brain Project reserves the right to stop providing access to resource in case of misconduct at any point in time or absence of results evaluated on a yearly basis.


A test allocation is intended to be used for a short project (Up to 12 months, 5-20 million core hours for 1 year and 20 TiB of data) for testing and optimization of a new prototype, initiating a collaboration with other teams and testing application execution at scale.

A development allocation allows a reduced set of users to get used to BBP environment and do code porting and early prototype development. Access is provided for up to 12 months with a maximum of 5 million core hours and 5TiB of storage.

A production allocation is intended to be used for large (1-3 years with at least 20 million core hours/year) application using well established production software. Scaling and performance of the used application is considered a must. It is expected that granted production allocation will lead to high quality publications every year. A yearly report describing how the allocation has been used will be required.
